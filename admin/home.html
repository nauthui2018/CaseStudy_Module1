ABOUT US

Since 2016, I saw a clip to make watchstrap and immediately fallen in love with this field. After that, I learnt how to make the watchstrap and some small leather products.... Steps by steps....and up to present, I am making great items for you and for me.
For materials, I try to find out high quality leather source from famous and trust leather supplier in the world (AMTAN and PAN company in US for high and best quality alligator skin; Henglong in Singapore - I just use leathers from these companies for my Alligator watch strap, wallet and credit card holder... ; Zermatt , Alran leather, Mando paint, Fil au Chinois thread from France).
I believe that when you place an order, you will receive a luxury, duration and fashionable product from me!
For services, it is very important. You order one from me, you should receive a comfortable and satisfied services besides products. I promise that I will always stay behind my customers and your product will be in lifetime services from me - I think simple that: Try to best!!! Customer is friend!!!
Making with passion! Creating value for life! That is T&J Handmade Leather purpose!
